Definitions

PointSnap this app has been set up to support how images are collected to monitor vegetation condition change overtime. This app and supporting protocols are being delivered to benefit the NSW government agencies including the NSW Office of Environment and Heritage (OEH), the Biodiversity Conservation Trust and National Parks and Wildlife Services (NPWS).

Protocol three protocols have been developed for individuals wishing to set up and monitor vegetation condition using photopoint images or ground cover/canopy cover images. These protocols should be followed to increase the reliability and temporal comparability of the capture images.

Smart phone application, application or app is the application developed to allow the user to collect site information and/or capture photopoint, canopy cover and ground cover images.

Image repository this is where capture images and supporting information (image metadata, site attribute and user information) will be stored. This will be accessible to and managed by the NSW Office of Environment and Heritage.

Your responsibilities

Following the Photopoint protocol

Follow the instructions listed in the protocol. This includes obtaining permission to set up photopoints on parks, reserves, private land and other areas that are not owned by the individual taking the photopoints.

Following the canopy cover and ground cover protocol

Follow the instructions listed in the protocol. This includes obtaining permission to capture images on parks, reserves, private land and other areas that are not owned by the individual taking the images.
